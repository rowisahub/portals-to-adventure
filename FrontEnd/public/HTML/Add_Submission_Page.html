THE BASICS

Portals to Adventure and World's Largest RPGs are excited to announce the Secret Doors Contest!

Have you ever thought you could be a role-playing game creator? Well, now's your chance!

The Secret Doors Contest invites amateur writers to create a new bit of dungeon hiding behind a secret door somewhere in the World's Largest Dungeon. Fans will vote on all the contestants' submissions, finalists advance to a second round of fan votes, and the winners will receive awards, prize money, and get their secret door entries published alongside a bunch of professional industry writers in the Secret Doors, Volume 1 book that's part of the upcoming Platinum Edition of World's Largest Dungeon, coming out in 2025.

Entry to the Secret Doors Contest is free.

Entrants will need to provide:

  • A unique title for their Secret Door adventure
  • A written manuscript of their adventure for 5e
  • A map of their dungeon that lies beyond their secret door
  • A thumbnail image that will be displayed to fans as they view and vote
  • A video where they introduce themselves, their dungeon adventure concept, why they made it and why fans should vote for it

That's it!

Looking for some hints on how to get started and make a top-notch dungeon? Click the Helpful Hints button.

THE FAN VOTING ROUNDS: INITIAL AND FINALS

The Initial Voting Round begins with the launch of the Secret Doors Contest on April 13, 2025 and continues until July 2, 2025. Entrants can submit their adventures during this entire period. Fans can vote for all entrants' submissions during this period, at $1 per vote, with a maximum of 10 votes per submission per voter (this is where the ever-increasing prize money pool is generated; the more people who vote, the more money the winners take home!). Only the top 20 vote getters in the Initial Voting Round move on to the Final Voting Round

The Final Voting Round begins on July 3, 2025 and ends on July 17, 2025. Submissions during this period are closed. Vote totals for the 20 remaining entrants are reset to zero; and the top vote getters in the Final Voting Round get additional prizes.
